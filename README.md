# Retail Sales Analysis Using SQL

## Project Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends.

The analysis was performed using MySQL and advanced SQL concepts such as Joins, Subqueries, CTEs, Window Functions, Stored Procedures, and Stored Functions.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing products
* Discover high-value customers
* Evaluate regional performance
* Study the impact of discounts on profitability
* Generate business recommendations

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail sales transactions including:

* Order Information
* Customer Information
* Product Information
* Sales
* Quantity
* Discount
* Profit
* Region and State Details

## Business Questions Solved

1. What is the total sales revenue?
2. What is the total profit?
3. Which category generates the highest sales?
4. Which products generate the highest profit?
5. Who are the top customers?
6. Which regions perform best?
7. How do discounts affect profitability?
8. Which products are loss-making?
9. Who is the top customer in each region?
10. What are the top 3 products in every category?

---

## Key Insights

* Technology category generated the highest sales.
* Top customers contributed significantly to total revenue.
* Some products generated negative profit despite strong sales.
* Higher discounts reduced profitability.
* Regional sales performance varied significantly.
* A small number of customers generated above-average sales.

---

## Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories.
* Reduce excessive discounts on low-margin products.
* Reward top customers through loyalty programs.
* Improve performance in underperforming regions.
* Monitor loss-making products regularly.
